Pelagia Christ University Hostel

The Best choice for 2021

PG near Christ University Bangalore. Set in the centre of Koramangala near Christ University, Pelagia Hostels solely caters for the accommodation requirements of students of Christ university. The PG buildings houses over 300 students during term time and are located in the SG Palya area of Koramangala at just a few metres from each other. SG Palya is a lively area and is very popular among students. You couldn’t be better placed for easy access to shops, restaurants and cafés and Christ university main campus is just a 2 minute stroll away. All properties boasts a variety of room types from three sharing to single room to suit all types of accommodation requirements.

Over the past several years we have been working hard to ensure that we not only provide the best student experience but also the safest and most comfortable place for them to live and study. Our catering team carefully source fresh produce everyday from the local markets and the menu is rotated on a regular basis. Our chefs carry experience in excess of 20 years in traditional north and south Indian dishes. This means we provide high quality food, that is healthy, ethical and uses lots of fresh ingredients.
